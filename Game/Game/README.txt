-------------------------------- SQUORB --------------------------------

Welcome to Squorb!

Named because the evil orbs wish to skew your vision until your demise!

The plot of the game is that you must convert the evil squorbs into light
squorbs before they can make it to the end of the path.

If a squorb does make it to the end, the world darkens and you begin to
lose your vision. But if you manage to get a converted squorb to the end
the world lights up a little.


In technical terms: screen shrink, screen grow.

Look around simply by moving the mouse, and place a tower by clicking,
it's that simple!

P.S. Hit Esc to quit the game
